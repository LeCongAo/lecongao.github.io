Humble Beginnings

My Home Group is in the business of building businesses. Founded in 2005 by Jereme Kleven and Mark Hutchins, MHG has since given thousands of agents the platform to grow a real business through a culture of accountability and a collaborative environment. Today, MHG is the #1 fastest growing residential real estate brokerage in the country, the 2nd fastest in the nation, and has been named “A fastest growing company” by Inc 500 five years in a row. With over 13,000 transactions closed each month and over 5 billion dollars in sales in the first quarter of 2019, MHG is slated to dramatically change the industry as a whole.
